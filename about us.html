About Us

We are one of India’s leading integrated express logistics provider, operating the largest physical network of customer access points in the country. We offer a comprehensive range of technology-enabled logistics solutions to serve a wide spectrum of customers across diverse industry verticals.

We commenced operations in 1990 and were the first express logistics company in India to implement a network of entrepreneurs (Channel Partners) that operate the-track-flow branded Customer Access Points. Our Channel Partners support us in building relationships, generating business and providing services to customers.

We are a network of over 14,000 physical customer access points servicing over 14,000 pin codes, reaching approximately 96% of India’s population. Internationally, We extend our shipping services to more than 220 destinations across the world through our global delivery partners.

We have been able to leverage the experience and business relationships of our Promoters to go beyond domestic offerings to establish a global presence and the ability to offer international cross-border services in key markets.
